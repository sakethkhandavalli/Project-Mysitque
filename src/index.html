About Us

StaTwig is responsible for creating an innovative solution that uses Internet-of-things (IoT) and blockchain technology to provide real-time tamper-proof end-to-end tracking that identifies problems and inefficiencies in any supply chain.

What We Do

  • Cold-Chain is a temperature-controlled supply chain used for food, vaccines and biological materials.
  • During this process, human error, temperature excursions and illegal activity, among other factors, can affect the safety and efficacy of the product.
  • It represents a serious challenge for industries across the board, as the sheer scale and complexity of the supply chain, makes it nearly impossible to track a product all the way from manufacturer to customer.
  • The scale of this problem is monumental, accounting for billions of dollars being lost to inefficient cold-chain management, which increases the cost of the product for the end consumer, while reducing its safety and effectiveness.
